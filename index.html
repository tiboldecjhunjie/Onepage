Sports Studio is the entertainment industry’s leading sports service provider for wardrobe, props, casting and product placement. DESIGN

Mock Ups and Logo Design

Mock Ups

Our in-house design team can provide you with digital mock-ups of sports apparel, jerseys, and uniforms for approval. When we build custom uniforms from scratch, it’s helpful to have a visual rendering to ensure the delivery of the product you envisioned.

Logo Design

Sports Studio offers custom design services, whether you’d like to build logos from scratch or alter the font on uniforms. Customization can help set your production apart, guaranteeing a unique flair on the uniforms in your sports scenes.

CUSTOMIZATION

Our Customization and Alteration Services

If you’ve got a vision in mind, let’s create it together. We can build any sports garment from scratch. Whether you’re looking to customize ball caps, letterman’s jackets, youth soccer uniforms, football helmets or even tees for your wrap party, we can help.

Sports Studio can create custom sublimated jerseys, allowing for maximum creativity as designs are printed directly into a jersey. Sublimation is especially popular among soccer jerseys, and is gaining more and more popularity with lacrosse and even minor league baseball.

FITTINGS

Avoid Last Minute Wardrobe Issues

Sports Studio is happy to accompany your team to fittings—we can even host them in our warehouse. Our experts will work hand in hand with wardrobe to help fittings run as smoothly as possible for stylists and talent.

CASTING

Athlete Casting

PSports Studio Casting is the nation’s largest database of athletic actors. With over 19,000 athletes, we can help you cast players for any sport, at any age, anywhere in the United States. We cast for big brands like Nike, Under Armour, and Gatorade. Some notable films we’ve cast for include 42, The Dark Knight Rises, and Crooked Arrows.

ON SET SPORT-TECH

As an extension of our wardrobe services, our On Set Sport-Technicians can accompany production to set. Our Sport-Techs are seasoned sports wardrobe experts, there to outfit athletes and to ensure no small detail is overlooked. Beyond our knowledge of the game, we’re well versed in the sports entertainment industry. Having been on more than 100 sets, including highly demanding jobs for the NFL and MLB, we understand the nuances of sponsorship. Likewise, we know the value of flexibility and readiness on set.

SPORTS CHOREOGRAPHY AND COORDINATION

Sports Studio has partnered with some of the top sports coordinators in the industry. We work with both former and current professional athletes with a keen eye for athletics and authenticity. Our team will work with you to ensure that your talent is lining up correctly and running through the proper motions. Working with us will give your production an authentic sports look and feel on camera.
